Intune Compliance Report

Import your Intune compliance export to analyse device status, view per-device issue icons at a glance, filter by failure type, and get remediation guidance for every policy failure.

ⓘ How to export from Intune — step by step
Step 1
Open Intune Admin Centre
Go to intune.microsoft.com and sign in. You need at minimum the Read role for Device Compliance.
Step 2
Navigate to Compliance Monitor
Click Devices in the left nav → Monitor → Device compliance. For more detail also open Noncompliant devices.
Step 3
Export the CSV
Click the Export button at the top of the report. Save the .csv file then drop it into the import panel below.
